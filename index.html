Understanding Your Space and Needs

Measure Your Space Accurately

Before exploring the beautiful rug collection at The Rug Market Canada, start by measuring the space where the rug will go. Accurate measurements help avoid purchasing a rug that is too large or too small for your room.

Consider Foot Traffic and Usage

Think about how much foot traffic the rug will endure. High-traffic areas like hallways and entryways need durable, stain-resistant rugs, while low-traffic areas offer more flexibility for delicate designs. Washable rugs are excellent choices for homes with kids or pets.

Choosing the Right Style and Color

Match Your Decor Style

Your rug should complement your existing home decor. The Rug Market Canada has an extensive range that includes Moroccan rugs, high pile rugs, and low pile rugs, accommodating everything from modern to traditional aesthetics.

Consider Color Coordination

Select colors that harmonize with your room's palette. Neutral tones work best in busy rooms, while vibrant and bold rugs add life and energy to simple spaces.

Material Matters: What to Choose?

Natural Fibers vs Synthetic

Natural fibers like wool and cotton provide softness, durability, and warmth. Synthetic materials such as polypropylene are often more affordable and stain-resistant, making them practical for everyday use.

Allergies and Maintenance

Low pile rugs are better for those with allergies because they trap less dust and are easier to clean. The Rug Market Canada offers a range of hypoallergenic options to suit different needs.

Budgeting Your Rug Purchase

Set a Realistic Budget

Rugs vary greatly in price depending on size, material, and design complexity. Having a clear budget helps you focus your choices while ensuring you invest in a rug that suits your financial plan and quality expectations.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I know what size rug to buy for my living room?

Measure your seating area and choose a rug that fits under at least the front legs of your furniture to anchor the space effectively.

Are washable rugs durable?

Yes, washable rugs are designed for durability and easy maintenance, making them ideal for homes with children or pets.

What is the difference between high pile and low pile rugs?

High pile rugs have longer fibers and provide a softer feel and more warmth. Low pile rugs have shorter fibers, making them easier to clean and better suited for high-traffic areas.

Can I use a rug on a hardwood floor?

Absolutely! Rugs add comfort and protect hardwood floors from scratches and wear.
